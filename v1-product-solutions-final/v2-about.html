The story so far

Hi, I'm
Shruthi.

EdTech Product Manager. Former professor. Lifelong student. I've spent over a decade at the intersection of learning, technology, and human potential.

I started my career in a college classroom in Mysore — and somewhere in the middle of designing curricula, I became obsessed with the design of learning itself. That obsession took me into instructional design, deep into LMS implementations and assessment operations, and ultimately to solution design and product management.

Today, my operational background enables me to bridge the gap between institutional needs and scalable technology solutions. But the thread that runs through everything? Making learning more effective, more human, and more just.
